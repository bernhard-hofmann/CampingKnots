Camping Knots

  • Figure Eight

    A good stopper knot.

  • Square or Reef

    Good for joining equally thick ropes.

  • Sheet/Becket Bend

    Good for joining rope of unequal thickness.

  • Bowline

    Good for tying rope around you.

  • Clove Hitch

    Good for tying rope to a round post.

  • Prusik

    Moving knot that can put tension in a line.

  • Alpine Butterfly

    Good for hanging things on a tight line.
